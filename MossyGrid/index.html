How to play

1. Click on the "Start game" button.

2. Enter the name of both players.

3. Click on START GAME.

4. You will now be able to see the dashboard on your screen. Each square represents a position on the board.

5. To make your move, click on the box you want to place the tile on (usually "x" or "O").

6. The game will continue to alternate between players until one of them manages to make a straight line, either horizontally, vertically or diagonally.

7. If you win, it will be marked as a point.

8. If you win, congratulations!
